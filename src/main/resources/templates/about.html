Who Are We?

We are your assistant to find what you need, whether it's medicine or medical instruments! Do you have medicine you don't need anymore? Or medical instruments you don't use? Here we will help you find people who need them and search for your needs too! With our professional volunteers, you will know if the medicine you have is needed or not, and know what medicine you have to take, and get you one too! Join our community, and help others and benefit!
