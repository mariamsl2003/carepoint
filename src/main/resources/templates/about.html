About MediLink

MediLink is a mission-driven platform built to connect donors and recipients of medical supplies across communities. Our goal is to reduce waste, enhance accessibility to healthcare, and empower people to make a real difference.

We believe that every person deserves access to the medical resources they need. Whether you're looking to donate unused medical supplies or in urgent need of support, MediLink is here to bridge that gap securely and effectively.

Built by a passionate team of developers and healthcare professionals, MediLink promotes compassion, transparency, and verified healthcare interactions.
